Who We Are

About MBSA

MBSA is a student organization at the University of Wisconsin–Madison dedicated to supporting business-minded students from all backgrounds. We host resume workshops, interview prep sessions, speaker events, LinkedIn coaching, and social events to help our members grow personally and professionally.

📚

Professional Development

Resume workshops, interview prep, and LinkedIn coaching to set you up for success.

🌐

Cultural Connection

A welcoming community that celebrates diversity and multicultural perspectives in business.

🤝

Community

Social events and networking opportunities that build lasting connections across campus.
